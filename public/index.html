Welcome To Johnny Lo's MEDEX

We are one of the marijuana friendly hotels in Denver. As a marijuana friendly bed and breakfast in Denver, we offer marijuana dispensary tours, marijuana education classes in Denver and a micro-brew tour. Johnny Lo’s Medx (marijuana education destination escape) is a part-time marijuana friendly bed and breakfast style retreat in Denver. If you book one of our “almost all inclusive” packages, you will be given access to sample some of Colorado’s best marijuana from the best recreational dispensaries in town, get to take a marijuana dispensary tour in Denver, and go through helpful marijuana education classes in Denver that are fun, quick, and worth their weight in gold! Do all or none, it’s your excape! We are excited you’ve discovered us and hope you plan a trip to our facility soon.
